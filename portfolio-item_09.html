illustrations: artwork

freestyle art

Drawing, painting and designing have always been my favorite things to do. I grew up drawing and copying superheroes and evil villains from my favorite manga, anime and comic book series. Then as I got a little older, I switched to digital drawing tools and software to sketch, color, and finish my drawings on paper. Even today, I like to do freestyle illustrations depending on what mood I am in. I used to illustrate with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, but recently moved to Affinity Designer and Photo as my main platforms for illustrations, while I was using Medibang Paint Pro mainly for manga and anime drawings. For tablets, I have switched from a Wacom Intuos Pro 5 to a Huion Kamvas Pro. In this category you can find some examples of my artwork and illustrations.
