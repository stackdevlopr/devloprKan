design/dev: website

concept design

As I briefly mentioned on the home page, I am convinced that my interest in design stems from my childhood hobby: drawing anime, manga, and comics. When I discovered digital drawing and design tools and software, I was overwhelmed by the many ways to be creative: If you can imagine it, you can create it. I try to familiarize myself with a wide range of design tools: I started with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, and then added the very powerful alternatives like Medibang Paint Pro, Inskape, and Affinity Photo, Designer, and Publisher to my arsenal. In this section, you'll find some of the concepts I designed using a mix of the above platforms.
